WHO WE ARE...


As a 100% black, female-owned organisation, we embarked on a journey to revolutionise the business world through a lens less captured. On a mission to bridge the gaps between efficient and effectively run corporations, by developing a powerful and cohesive culture built on deep trust and mutual understanding. We present to you the future of business. A future that's free of discriminatory policies that hinder the drive of the overlooked workforce. Here, we learn to understand the needs of the people who harness and mould the beautiful future that lies ahead. Solidify your ability to continue in an ever-changing business climate, by trusting in us to drive you to become the most sustainable version of yourself.

Quintessential Consulting Group subscribes to a principled approach to business and aligns its clients, management and work force with this approach. QE believes that transformation cannot be imposed from the outside, but has to be driven from within and form part of the value system of organisations. We follow a project management approach with a 7 step intervention process. If done well, this structured approach ensures a sustainable and profitable organisation that creates exceptional value for its people and the community in which it operates. Before embarking on this process, we prepare the organisation fully for this process (read more: critical success factors), and provide ongoing support throughout the process of implementation, monitoring and evaluation.
